Can I change my plan later?

Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through the member portal or at the front desk. Upgrades take effect immediately.

Are there any hidden signup fees?

Transparency is key. We have a one-time activation fee of $25 for all new memberships to cover system setup and your digital access key.

What is the "Recovery Zone"?

Our Elite exclusive area featuring cryotherapy tanks, infrared saunas, and hydro-massage beds designed to accelerate muscle recovery and reduce inflammation.

Do sessions roll over each month?

Personal training sessions for Pro members expire at the end of the billing cycle. Elite members have unlimited access, so rollover is never an issue!
